Revenue & invoicing

Vachi.ai automates billing, collections, and payments with its Accounts Receivable Agent while the Invoice Automation Agent processes invoices and flags exceptions. The Bullmate Receipt Manager securely captures client receipts, speeding up revenue cycles and freeing teams for strategic decisions.

Close management

Month-end and quarter-end closes are simplified with MIP Automation extracting GL data, GL Analysis providing insights, and Integrated Payroll handling payroll posting and ledger updates. Vachi.ai reduces errors and allows accountants to focus on high-value financial tasks.

Investor & GAAP reporting

SOC-1 Audit Control Reports are generated automatically while GL vs TB Comparison identifies discrepancies. Tax Package Generator and related agents consolidate engagement letters, RRF forms, and tax deliverables, delivering accurate, compliant, and transparent reporting for investors and stakeholders.
