Vachi.ai is a multi-layered AI agent ecosystem built from decades of CPA expertise serving a diverse client base: Tribal Entities, Universities, Housing Authorities, School Districts, Cities, and more. It is a digital colleague workforce designed specifically for audit, accounting, and tax workflows, providing strategic foresight and ensuring audit-ready compliance across varied financial structures.
By deploying specialized AI agents across every workflow—from GL analysis to tax engagement letters—Vachi.ai delivers 10x efficiency and 99%+ accuracy. This frees your human staff from repetitive burdens, allowing them to focus on high-value, judgment-intensive advisory, strategy, and client impact.
For executives, Vachi.ai moves beyond mere automation. It provides Financial Intelligence Agents for real-time risk prediction, Trend Analysis of Indirect Costs, and Revenue Recognizers to ensure full governance visibility and board-ready insights across all subsidiaries and complex entities.
Unlike generic AI, Vachi.ai is trained directly from the workflows of a CPA firm with experience in GASB, Tribal, NPO, and Special District reporting. Our agents are pre-programmed to understand the nuances of compliance for clients like Charter Schools, Housing Authorities, and County entities, ensuring robust regulatory adherence from day one.
